## Safety Resources

### Riding Safety
- [Kids Protective Gear Guide](https://aridejunkie.com/kids-protective-gear-guide-electric-scooter/) - Essential safety equipment for young riders.
- [CDC E-Scooter Safety](https://www.cdc.gov/transportationsafety/escooter/index.html) - Safety data and recommendations from the CDC.

### Protective Gear
- Always wear a certified helmet (CPSC, ASTM, or Snell rated)
- Gloves - Protect hands in case of falls
- Knee/Elbow Pads - Especially important for beginners
- Visibility Gear - Reflective clothing and lights for night riding

## Safety & Best Practices

### Before You Ride
- Check tire pressure
- Test brakes
- Ensure lights work
- Charge battery adequately
- Wear protective gear

### While Riding
- Follow traffic laws
- Ride defensively
- Use bike lanes when available
- Signal turns
- Avoid riding in rain (if scooter not rated for it)

### Storage & Care
- Store indoors when possible
- Keep battery charged between 20-80% for longevity
- Clean regularly
- Check bolts and connections monthly
